Main Info

In Shotokan you will be required to take an exam to gain the next grade (kyu/dan), you will be graded apon a few different aspects. Kata Kumite Moves/Techniques Kata – Originally were teaching and training methods by which successful combat techniques were preserved and passed on. They are performed against a series of predefined imaginary attacks by several opponents. There are a total of 27 kata in the current Shotokan System. Kumite – The first type of kumite for beginners is Gohon kumite. The defender steps back each time, blocking the attacks and performing a counterattack (usally with a Kiai) after the last block. Other Kumite can include, Ippon kumite – one step sparring, Sanbon kumite – three step sparring, Jiyu kumite – free sparring.

3rd Kyu (Brown belt) 3rd Kyu is the low intermediate shotokan rank where you will learn some more techniques and higher kata, to attain your next Kyu you will be asked to preform Some Techniques, Kumite and preform a Kata. Brown Belt (2nd Kyu)

2nd Kyu is the mid intermediate shotokan rank where you will learn some more techniques and higher kata, to attain your next Kyu you will be asked to preform Some Techniques, Kumite and preform two Katas. Techniques ZENKUTSU DACHI GEDAN BARAI MAE GERI – JUN ZUKI MAWASHI GERI – GYAKU ZUKI GYAKU ZUKI – MAEGERI – JUN ZUKI – GEDAN BARAI ZENKUTSU DACHI GAMAE MAE GERI – MAWASHI GERI KIZAMI YOKO KEKOMI – USHIRO GERI KIBA DACHI YOKO KEAGE – YOKO KEKOMI The Kumite The kumite will be Jiyu Kihon Ippon where you will be asked to preform your techniques on a partner “Jodan” & “Chudan” will be called meaning Jodan “head” or Chudan “chest” on calling the body part you will defend or attack that part of the body The Kata The Kata you will be asked to preform and be graded on is Tekki Shodan plus and extra kata from the Heian katas from previous Kyu Grades, below is a short Video of Sensei Kanazawa preforming Tekki Shodan as a reminder, you can also look back on previous Heian Katas by looking through the previous Kyu Grades.
